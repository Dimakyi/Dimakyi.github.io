About Us

Welcome to Coffee Queue-Free, an innovative solution created by a passionate young woman who saw the need to make mornings easier for people everywhere. This app was built by Dimakatso Masake, a dedicated WethinkCode student and a proud Black woman with a vision to simplify the daily routine for coffee lovers.

As someone who understands the hustle of busy mornings and the importance of supporting local businesses, Dimakatso developed this app to provide a seamless coffee ordering experience. The idea for Coffee Queue-Free came from the frustration of waiting in long lines just to grab a cup of coffee. She wanted to create a solution that would save time and allow people to enjoy their mornings without the stress of waiting.

The Vision

The goal behind Coffee Queue-Free is simple: make mornings easier, more efficient, and support local businesses along the way. By allowing users to order ahead, customize their drinks, and pick them up at a designated time, this app helps you skip the line and get your favorite coffee faster — all while supporting independent cafes and coffee shops in your area.

As a young woman with a desire to bring positive change, Dimakatso is proud to offer this tool that empowers individuals to take control of their mornings while promoting the success of small businesses. It’s not just about coffee — it’s about creating a better start to the day, fostering community, and ensuring that no one has to choose between good coffee and a busy schedule.
